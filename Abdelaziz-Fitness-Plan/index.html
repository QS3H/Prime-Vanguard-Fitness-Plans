Training Philosophy

The Prime Vanguard Approach to Physical Mastery

Why a 4-Day Upper/Lower Split?

As an intermediate lifter, this 4-day split is the most efficient path to recomposition—burning fat while stimulating muscle growth:

✓ Strength + Hypertrophy
Two upper and two lower days target both strength and muscle building
✓ Visceral Fat Attack
Post-workout incline walks + dedicated Zone 2 cardio day
✓ Recovery Built In
Active recovery day and full rest day protect your joints and CNS
✓ Progressive Overload
Log every weight and rep—lifting more over time builds muscle armor

The Prime Vanguard Rules for Execution

  1. The Incline Walk Rule: During the 20-minute post-workout walk, do not hold the treadmill handles. Holding on reduces calorie burn by up to 30% and ruins your posture. Let your arms swing.
  2. Progressive Overload: If you benched 60kg for 8 reps this week, next week try 62.5kg for 8 reps, or 60kg for 9 reps. If the numbers aren't going up, you aren't building muscle.
  3. The Nutrition Mandate: You cannot out-train a bad diet. Eat ~2,100–2,200 kcal per day with a minimum of 160–180g of protein.
  4. Sleep is the Anabolic Steroid: Your body recovers at night. If you sleep 5 hours, this program will fail. Get 7–8 hours.

Day 6: Dedicated Cardio (Visceral Fat Destroyer)

Your VFI is 14.9. This day is non-negotiable. We are targeting Zone 2 cardio to burn pure fat without spiking cortisol.

Option A: The Steady State Burn (Recommended)
Activity: Incline treadmill walking, Stairmaster, or cycling.
Duration: 45–60 minutes.
Intensity: Zone 2 — able to hold a conversation, but sweating and breathing heavily (heart rate roughly 120–140 BPM).

Option B: The HIIT Finisher (If short on time)
Activity: Rowing machine, Assault Bike, or treadmill sprints.
Protocol: 20 minutes total — 30 seconds ALL-OUT sprint, 90 seconds slow recovery. Repeat 10 times.

The Prime Vanguard Execution Rules

  1. The 80/20 Rule: Eat this exact plan 80% of the time. If you have a family gathering or Eid, eat the halal food, enjoy it, and get right back on track at the very next meal. No guilt, just execution.

  3. Sunday Meal Prep: Spend 2 hours on Sunday. Boil 10 eggs, grill your 2kg of chicken, and chop your vegetables. If your food is ready, you won't order delivery when you're tired.
