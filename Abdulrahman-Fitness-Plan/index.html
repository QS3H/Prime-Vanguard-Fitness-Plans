Training Philosophy

The Prime Vanguard Approach to Physical Mastery

Why Full Body Workouts?

As a beginner at 105 kg, full body training 3x/week is the most efficient path to your goals:

✓ Maximizes Fat Loss
More muscles engaged = more calories burned per session
✓ Faster Skill Learning
Practice movements 3x/week vs 1x/week
✓ Joint Protection
Lower volume per session, better recovery
✓ Hormonal Optimization
Compound lifts boost testosterone & growth hormone

The 5 Non-Negotiable Rules

  1. Form Over Ego: Perfect technique is non-negotiable. Leave your ego at the door.
  2. Progressive Overload: Every week, add 1-2 reps or increase weight slightly.
  3. Rest Periods: 60-90 seconds between sets. Use a timer.
  4. Hydration: 4 liters of water daily. Your metabolism depends on it.
  5. Consistency: Never miss two workouts in a row. Ever.

The 5 Non-Negotiable Rules

  1. Never Skip Protein: Even if you skip carbs, never skip eggs, ful, or gibna. Your 42.8 kg of muscle depends on hitting 185g protein daily.
  2. Hydration is Free: Drink 4 liters of water daily. This is critical for fat loss and metabolism.
  3. Track Everything: Use MyFitnessPal or a notebook. If you don't track, you won't know if you're in a deficit.
  4. Sunday Meal Prep: Boil 21 eggs, cook 1kg ful and 500g lentils, chop vegetables. This saves time and prevents bad decisions.
  5. The 80/20 Rule: Eat this plan 80% of the time. If you have a family gathering, enjoy it, then return to the plan immediately.

Smart Shopping Hacks

✓ Shop Smart: Local souqs offer the freshest produce. Buy vegetables late in the afternoon for the best selection.

✓ Protein Maximizers: Use 1 egg yolk + 2 egg whites per serving to maximize protein while managing fats. Ful + eggs = complete amino acids.

✓ Seasonal Swaps: Winter: Oranges, guava. Summer: Watermelon, mango. Year-round: Cabbage, carrots, onions.
